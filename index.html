About Us

As part Of the global Omdena community, our chapter is dedicated to leveraging the power of Al to address real-world challenges. With a focus on collaboration, compassion, curiosity, and consciousness, we work together to develop innovative solutions that make a positive impact. From our Water Quality Monitoring project, utilizing satellite imagery to ensure the safety of water sources, to our Road Inspection Project, revolutionizing road condition management, our chapter has already showcased its commitment to driving meaningful change. Join us as we continue to grow and create a better future through the application of Al. Together, we can make a difference!
